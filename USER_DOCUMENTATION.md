# Invoice Maker - Complete User Documentation

Welcome to the comprehensive user guide for your Invoice Maker SaaS platform. This documentation is designed for **Business Owners** and **Team Members** to understand and utilize the full potential of the platform.

*(Note: Super Administration functionalities are excluded from this guide.)*

---

## 1. Getting Started & Dashboard
When you log in, you are greeted by the **Dashboard**. This is your central hub for business insights.
- **Financial Overview:** View total outstanding amounts, monthly recurring revenue, and total expenses.
- **AI-Powered Insights:** Click the **"Generate AI Insights"** button on the dashboard to get a personalized, AI-driven analysis of your recent financial performance based on your invoices and expenses.
- **Recent Activity:** Quickly access your most recently created invoices, expenses, and team activities.

## 2. Business Profile & Branding
Customize how your business appears to your clients.
- **Settings > Business Profile:**
  - **Company Details:** Set your company name, logo, address, and contact information.
  - **Brand Colors:** Choose a custom brand color. This color will be dynamically injected into all public links, your client portal, and the PDF invoices you send out.
  - **B2B Network Sync:** Opt-in to the "Accept Network Invoices" feature. If enabled, whenever another business on the platform sends you an invoice, it will automatically appear in your Expenses tab without any manual entry!
  - **Payment Terms:** Define default payment terms (e.g., Net 15, Net 30) for your invoices.

## 3. Team Management
Collaborate with your employees or accountants seamlessly.
- **Settings > Team:**
  - **Invite Members:** Send an email invitation to a new team member. 
  - **Roles:** Assign permissions (e.g., Admin, Member).
  - **Collaboration:** Team members share the same business workspace, clients, and financial records.

## 4. Client Management
Keep all your customer data organized.
- **Clients Tab:**
  - **Add a Client:** Store billing addresses, VAT numbers, and contact emails.
  - **Client Portal Link:** Every client gets a secure, private Client Portal where they can view their invoice history and interact with your business.

## 5. Products & Services (Inventory)
Manage the items you frequently bill for.
- **Products Tab:**
  - Create services or physical products with default prices and tax rates.
  - **Stock Management:** If enabled, creating an invoice for a physical product will automatically deduct the billed quantity from your available stock.

## 6. Invoicing Engine
The core of your business. Create stunning, professional invoices in seconds.
- **Invoices Tab > Create:**
  - Select a client, add products/services from your dropdown, and apply taxes or discounts.
  - **Recurring Invoices:** Set an invoice to automatically generate and send (Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly).
  - **PDF Generation:** Download a pixel-perfect PDF version of the invoice instantly.
  - **Public Link:** Share a secure web link with your client. They can view the invoice online, download it, and leave comments.
  - **Online Payments (Stripe):** If a Stripe key is configured, clients can click "Pay Now" on the public link to pay via Credit Card/Apple Pay. The system will automatically mark the invoice as "Paid".

## 7. Estimates / Quotes
Send proposals before finalizing a sale.
- **Estimates Tab:**
  - Similar flow to Invoices, but represents a quote.
  - **Convert to Invoice:** Once a client approves the estimate (which they can do via the public link), you can convert it directly into an active Invoice with one click.

## 8. Expense Management
Track where your money is going.
- **Expenses Tab:**
  - **Create Expense:** Log purchases, select tracking categories (e.g., IT, Marketing), and input custom expense numbers (or use the auto-generated ones).
  - **Direct Attribution:** Link an expense directly to a specific Invoice (e.g., shipping costs for a specific order).
  - **Attachments:** Upload multiple PDF receipts or images to justify the expense. Built-in PDF preview allows you to view the receipts directly in the app.

## 9. Accounting & Cash Book
Your complete financial ledger.
- **Accounting > Cash Book:**
  - Lists every single cash flow event (Income from Paid Invoices, Outbound logic from Expenses).
  - **AI Health Check:** Get specific AI insights generated exclusively on your cash book health.
  - **Exports:** Export your ledger to **Excel/CSV** or **PDF** to hand off to your accountant.

- **Bank Reconciliation (CSV Import):**
  - Found inside the Cash Book tab.
  - **Upload:** Upload your raw bank statement (CSV format).
  - **Map:** Tell the system which column is the Date, Description, and Amount.
  - **Auto-Match:** The system utilizes smart keyword algorithms to pair imported bank lines with your unpaid Invoices (Income) and unlinked Expenses.
  - **Import:** Push the matched entries straight into your ledger.

## 10. Client Portal & Support Tickets
Offer your customers a premium experience.
- **Client Portal:**
  - Clients receive a secure "Magic Link" to access their portal.
  - They can view their lifetime statement, download bulk invoices, and see their outstanding balance.
- **Support Tickets:**
  - Inside the portal, clients can open a Support Ticket (e.g., "Question about recent bill").
  - **Business View:** You can view these tickets inside your dashboard under the "Client Support" section and reply directly.

## 11. Custom Templates Builder
Make your invoices look exactly how you want.
- **Templates Tab:**
  - Use the drag-and-drop Template Builder to construct new invoice layouts.
  - Switch between standard layouts, minimalist layouts, or dynamic modern layouts.

## 12. Security & User Profile
Keep your personal account secure.
- **Profile:**
  - Switch your interface language directly from your profile.
  - **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable 2FA to require an authenticator app code when logging in, highly recommended for protecting financial data.
