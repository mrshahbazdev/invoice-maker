# Getting Started with Invoice Maker

Welcome to the comprehensive user guide for your Invoice Maker SaaS platform. This documentation is designed for Business Owners and Team Members to understand and utilize the full potential of the platform.

When you log in, you are greeted by the Dashboard. This is your central hub for business insights.

## Financial Overview
The Dashboard displays your total outstanding amounts, monthly recurring revenue, and total expenses at a glance. It gives you an immediate picture of your business's financial health.

## AI-Powered Insights
Click the "Generate AI Insights" button on the dashboard to get a personalized, AI-driven analysis of your recent financial performance based on your invoices and expenses. The AI will summarize trends and highlight areas needing attention.

## Recent Activity
Quickly access your most recently created invoices, expenses, and team activities directly from the dashboard, allowing you to jump right back into your workflow.

## Managing Your Team
Collaborate with your employees or accountants seamlessly under the **Settings > Team** section.
- **Invite Members:** Send an email invitation to a new team member.
- **Roles:** Assign permissions (e.g., Admin, Member) to restrict or grant access to sensitive financial data.
- **Collaboration:** Team members share the same business workspace, clients, and financial records, ensuring everyone is on the same page.
